Getting Started

Welcome to our app! Here's a quick guide to help you get started:

Required steps:

1. Calculate Calorie Goal and Resting Burned Calories

Click the "Calculate Calorie Goal and Resting Burned Calories" button. Enter the required information and click "Calculate." The calorie goal is the daily calorie intake needed for a calorie deficit. Burned calories at rest indicate the number of calories your body expends while at rest, including during sleep. Recalculate if your weight changes.

2. Add Foods on the "Foods" page

Go to the "Foods" page to add your most used foods. Include the calorie information per 100 grams and add a picture if you'd like. These foods can then be used in your recipes and meals.

3. Add My Recipes on the "My Recipes" page

Go to the "My Recipes" page to add your favorite recipes. When creating a recipe, you can add ingredients from your food library. These recipes can then be added to your meals.

4. Add Foods And Recipes on the "Meals" page

On the Meals page, you can add foods and recipes with their quantities. The app will calculate the total calories for your meal.

Optional steps:

1. Edit your Account

Click on the "Edit profile" button and follow the instructions.

2. Add Current Weight

Click "Add Current Weight" button to log your current weight every day. Enter your weight in kilograms and click "Submit."

3. Add Activity

To add an activity, click on the "Add Activity" button. Enter the duration and activity type to calculate the burned calories.

4. Daily Statistics

On the "Daily Statistics" page you can view your calorie goal, weight, consumed calories and burned calories for the past 30 days.

5. Activities

On the "Activities" page you can view duration, activity type and burned calories about your activities for the past 30 days.
